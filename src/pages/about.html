About Me

Harley Martin-Farrell

Hi! I'm Harley, an amateur web developer with a passion for roleplaying and online games, currently studying full stack development in Brisbane.

Over the years, I've completed a number of minor certificates in IT and Media and I'm hoping to use the full-stack development skills I'm learning at the moment to break into the industry for a new career.

When I'm not spending hours perfecting a navigation button for a website most people won't see on mobile, I like to kick back and play some chill video games, or get some mates around and bring something to the table. I am also a roleplaying game enthusiast, who loves figuring out how to use different frameworks to tell collaborative stories.
